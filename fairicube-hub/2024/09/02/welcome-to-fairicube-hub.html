Introducing the FAIRiCUBE Hub

Sep 2, 2024

The FAIRiCUBE Hub—a unifying platform for discovering and utilizing F.A.I.R. (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable) datacubes—is now live! The Hub’s goal is to empower researchers, data scientists, and industry experts with standardized, analysis-ready datacubes that are fully compliant with F.A.I.R. principles.

FAIRiCUBE Hub is a unifying platform for discovering and utilizing F.A.I.R. (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable) datacubes. Our goal is to empower researchers, data scientists, and industry experts with standardized, analysis-ready datacubes that are fully compliant with F.A.I.R. principles.

This project has received funding from the Horizon Europe programme under grant agreement No 101059238.

Created in project EU FAIRiCUBE, implemented by Constructor University L-SIS research group.
